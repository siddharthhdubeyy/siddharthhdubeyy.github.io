About me

It started with a random Python tutorial on YouTube when I was 11. What began as curiosity grew into something I couldn't put down — the architecture of intelligent systems, how machines reason, how language and logic intersect at scale.

"Software is ultimately an abstraction of mathematics — patterns, logic, and structure brought to life."

I've been freelancing since I was 14 — building AI-powered chatbots, automation workflows, and tools for real clients. I don't wait to be fully prepared. I build, I learn, I iterate.

Beyond the technical side, I'm drawn to mathematics and content — writing and explaining the ideas I'm working through. AI isn't a trend to me. It's the most interesting engineering problem of our time.
